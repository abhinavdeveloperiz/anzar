About

Who Is Dr. Anzar Azeez?

Dr. Anzar Azeez is a distinguished educationist, medical professional, and visionary leader. As the Director of the Azeezia Group of Institutions and Delhi Public School, he has dedicated his career to advancing quality education, healthcare, and community development.

With a passion for academic excellence and holistic education, Dr. Azeez has been instrumental in guiding institutions that champion innovation, discipline, and student empowerment. Under his leadership, the institutions continue to promote modern learning practices and value-based development.

His dedication to community development reflects his vision of shaping responsible, knowledgeable, and globally competent individuals who will lead tomorrow's world.
